Frequently Asked Questions

Clearing up the main points so you can start with total confidence.

Does this really work for mature skin with wrinkles and sagging?

Yes! SKINNEW.AI was developed specifically with the needs of skin from 35-40 years and older in mind. The AI analyzes factors like collagen loss, firmness, and hydration to create a plan focused on rejuvenation, smoothing fine lines, and restoring skin's radiance.

Are the recommended products safe? I have sensitive skin.

Absolutely. One of the questions in our diagnosis is about your skin's sensitivity. Based on your answer, the AI prioritizes gentle, hypoallergenic ingredients and also offers options for homemade, natural recipes, ensuring your care plan is both effective and safe for you.

Will I have to spend a lot on the recommended products?

Not at all! One of our biggest differentiators is affordability. SKINNEW.AI is trained to find the "hidden gems" of drugstores: products with great value and powerful active ingredients. Additionally, we always offer alternatives with homemade recipes using ingredients you likely already have.

I don't have much time. Is the skincare routine long or complicated?

We understand the daily rush. That's why the routines are direct and optimized, taking only a few minutes in the morning and at night. The PDF guide explains the step-by-step process clearly and simply, so you can take care of your skin without messing up your schedule.

Is this a subscription or a one-time payment?

It's a one-time payment. You pay once and get access to your personalized plan for the contracted period (2 months for the Basic plan or forever for the Complete plan). There are no monthly fees, hidden charges, or any kind of recurring billing. Simple, transparent, and no surprises on your statement.
